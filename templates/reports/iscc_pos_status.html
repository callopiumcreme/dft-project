3. Commitment — Post-acceptance Delivery

{{ submitter_company }}, in concert with {{ off_taker }} (UK obligated supplier of record), hereby commits to deliver to the {{ regulator }} the formal retrospective ISCC EU Proofs of Sustainability for the {{ period_label }} input volumes covered by this submission within thirty (30) calendar days of formal DfT acceptance of the {{ submission_ref }} bundle.

Where the certifier ultimately declines to issue a retrospective PoS for a given collecting point, {{ submitter_company }} commits to (i) documenting the refusal with the certifier's written statement, (ii) engaging the fallback non-ISCC evidence procedure described in the UK RTFO Guidance Part Two (Carbon and Sustainability), and (iii) notifying the Unit within five (5) working days of receipt of the refusal.

Crown Oil Limited further offers, on a contingent basis and at the Unit's discretion, to engage an independent ISCC verifier (Bureau Veritas Colombia or SGS Colombia) for a pre-audit of the retrospective PoS evidence pack once issued. Engagement can be initiated within five (5) working days of Unit confirmation.
